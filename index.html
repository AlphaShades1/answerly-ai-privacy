Answerly AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

What We Collect

Answerly AI does not collect any personally identifiable information. The only data stored is your activation code and session token, saved locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.

What We Don't Collect

  • No name, email, or address
  • No browsing history
  • No quiz content or answers
  • No financial information

Third Party Services

Stripe processes payments. See Stripe's privacy policy. Google Gemini AI processes quiz questions to generate answers. No personally identifiable information is sent.
